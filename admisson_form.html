Admission Criteria

We welcome applications for students from Kindergarten to Grade 10. Below are the admission criteria:

  • Age Requirements: Students must meet the minimum age requirement for their respective grade level.
  • Previous Academic Records: Submission of previous school records and report cards.
  • Entrance Test: An entrance test may be required for certain grades.
  • Personal Interview: A personal interview with the student and parent may be scheduled.

Admission Process

Follow these steps to apply for admission:

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit required documents (birth certificate, report cards, etc.).
  3. Pay the application fee.
  4. Attend the entrance test (if applicable).
  5. Participate in the personal interview.
  6. Receive admission decision via email.
